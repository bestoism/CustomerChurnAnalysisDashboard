## ğŸ¯ Business Problems

The analysis is structured to answer key business questions, moving from a high-level overview to specific, actionable recommendations:

1.  **Overall Churn Landscape:** What is the overall churn rate and what are the basic profiles of churning customers?
2.  **Root Cause Identification:** How do contract types and the availability of support services influence churn?
3.  **Strategic Formulation:** How does the number of services impact loyalty, and what is the estimated financial loss due to churn?
4.  **Priority Segments:** Who are the high-value, high-risk customers that need immediate attention?
